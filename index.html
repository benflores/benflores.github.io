About

Ben Flores is a stand-up comedian in Boston, Massachusetts. Ben's comedy welcomes you with open arms into absurd, misanthropic twists on his personal life. He performs regularly around Boston and is happy to travel farther if you'll let him sling his jokes at you.

Come hurl your judgment upon him at one of the shows listed here!
